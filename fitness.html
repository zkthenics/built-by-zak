Fitness - Maximise your physical potential

Programs ↓

Push

1. Weighted Dips

4 sets × 6–8 reps

Rest: 90–120 sec

Tip: Lean forward slightly to target the chest more; keep elbows at ~45°

2. Incline Barbell Bench Press

4 sets × 6–10 reps

Rest: 90–120 sec

Tip: Focus on a controlled eccentric and full range of motion

3. Seated Dumbbell Shoulder Press

3 sets × 8–10 reps

Rest: 60–90 sec

Tip: Keep core tight, press evenly through both arms

4. Cable Chest Fly (Mid or Low)

3 sets × 10–12 reps

Rest: 45–60 sec

Tip: Squeeze at the top, slow eccentric

5. Lateral Raises

3 sets × 12–15 reps

Rest: 30–45 sec

Tip: Use controlled motion; avoid swinging

6. Overhead Cable Triceps Extensions

3 sets × 10–12 reps

Rest: 45–60 sec

Tip: Keep elbows fixed; full stretch and contraction

Pull

1. Muscle-Ups

3–4 sets × 3–6 reps

Rest: 2–3 min

Tip: Explosive pull; focus on smooth transition over the bar

2. Weighted Pull-Ups

4 sets × 6–8 reps

Rest: 90–120 sec

Tip: Full range of motion; control both the pull and descent

3. Barbell Bent-Over Rows

4 sets × 8–10 reps

Rest: 90 sec

Tip: Keep your back flat and squeeze shoulder blades at the top

4. Chest-Supported T-Bar Row

3 sets × 10–12 reps

Rest: 60–90 sec

Tip: Focus on pulling with your lats, not your arms

5. Face Pulls (Cable or Bands)

3 sets × 12–15 reps

Rest: 45–60 sec

Tip: Elbows high and wide, squeeze rear delts

6. Barbell or Dumbbell Curls

3 sets × 10–12 reps

Rest: 45–60 sec

Tip: Slow negative for better biceps engagement

Legs

1. Barbell Back Squats

4–5 sets × 5–8 reps

Rest: 2–3 min

Tip: Break at the hips first, squat to depth, and drive through your heels

2. Romanian Deadlifts (Barbell or Dumbbell)

4 sets × 8–10 reps

Rest: 90–120 sec

Tip: Slight knee bend; focus on hamstring stretch and glute squeeze

3. Walking Lunges (Dumbbells or Bodyweight)

3 sets × 20 steps total

Rest: 60–90 sec

Tip: Keep chest upright; knee should not go beyond toes

4. Leg Press

3 sets × 10–12 reps

Rest: 90 sec

Tip: Push through the mid-foot; do not lock out knees at the top

5. Leg Curls (Seated or Lying)

3 sets × 12–15 reps

Rest: 45–60 sec

Tip: Squeeze the contraction, pause briefly at the top

6. Standing Calf Raises

4 sets × 12–15 reps

Rest: 30–45 sec

Tip: Full range of motion; slow eccentric
